City University College ( CUC ) is a Qatari-owned university college and part of Artan Holding, owners of the highly successful Doha British School Ain Khaled and Wakra and Qatar Skills Academy. The new college will offer specialist business courses in Accounting and Finance, Business Management, Human Resource Management, Marketing, Business Operations and Entrepreneurship and Small Business Management. All our teaching programmes are delivered by highly qualified, English speaking faculty from the UK, Canada, New Zealand and India ensuring the most up to date and expert teaching is available to our students.

vision

To be the leading educational institution for vocational higher education in the region by delivering innovative and reflective teaching, training and career support.

mission

Our mission is to provide students with access to bespoke higher education routes to university and the workplace, developing their practical and reflective skills. We aim to have an immediate impact on our students’ learning, in their workplaces and in their lives.

teachings and learning values

Our HNC and HND programmes offered provides a strong foundation of analytical and realworld, industry-specific skills that will give you a career advantage. Our students achieve the standards required by the international awarding body and the University of Portsmouth to learn the qualitative and quantitative concepts in their subject fields and apply it to their respected industries through practical projects, assignments, case studies, quizzes and tests, formal assessments, presentations, self-study and research.

  • That our students achieve the standards required by international study.

That our students achieve competence in the performance of a range of practical tasks as part of their studies.

That our students master the skills and the knowledge integral to a learner-centric approach.
