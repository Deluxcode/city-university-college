our programme structure

City University College offers year 12 and year 13 high school graduates credit-based programmes that provide pathways to a business-related university degree and skilled employment.

The programmes offered are externally awarded by the international awarding body Pearson and recognised by our international partner, Portsmouth University:

Foundation year

BTEC Level 4 Higher National Certificate (HNC) – equivalent to first year of a UK bachelor 3

BTEC Level 5 Higher National Diploma (HND) – equivalent to second year of a UK bachelor degree

Why choose to study a BTEC?

BTEC stands for the Business and Technology Education Council in the United Kingdom. BTECs are specialist work-related qualifications. They combine practical learning with subject content and academic theory.

our btecs are

Globally recognized

BTECs are awarded by the international body, Pearson, and accepted by the workforce and over 700 universities worldwide.

Effective

Students build competencies for the real world that gain them jobs and recognition at work.

Skills-based

Our personalised learning plans are developed through continuous portfolio assessments, allowing each student to build a unique set of skills.

Flexible

Full-time and part-time options. Our programmes are credit-based and therefore flexible. We may also recognise prior learning.

Hands-on

Designed to attract students who prefer a more practical and applied learning style.

Learner-centred

We put each individual student first. We are partners with several universities including Portsmouth University and our lecturers will coach you through your career choices and help you apply.

Networked

We develop working relationships between our students and prospective employers and institutions

BTEC Higher National Certificate (HNC) Level 4 is a one year, full-time programme offering students a more detailed understanding of the business topic areas comprised of core courses and optional units. The optional units represent a wider range of occupational sectors at Level 4.

Level 4 courses are designed to focus on the basic but solid knowledge of the core business areas and prepare you for specialization in Level 5’s Higher National Diploma (HND)

An IELTS Preparation course is available as an elective evening course for those who require to achieve a higher English score at an additional charge.

BTEC Higher National Diploma (HND) Level 5 is a one year, full-time programme offering students six specialist study options designed to help students develop into their chosen occupational areas or to progress towards a bachelor’s degree at City University College’s partner – the University of Portsmouth – or any institute of their choice.

The specialist options are aligned with professional body standards and will assist in equipping students with industry-specific specific skills, providing professional status and routes to employment.

There is also a General Business option that allows students to cover course content without specializing at this stage.

The importance of English

All of our courses are taught in English and it is necessary that students are able to use English language effectively within an academic context. This means students need good levels of reading, analysis and formal writing as well as listening and speaking skills.

You will need to have achieved an IELTS score of 5.5 or equivalent to enter a BTEC Level 4 course, and to achieve an IELTS score of 6.0 or equivalent before you can enter to study for a university degree.

If you do not have an IELTS qualification at 5.5, you should apply for the foundation course to help you to achieve the entry standard for Level 4. English Language within an academic and business context is an essential part of our course provision and we make available, at an additional charge, preparation for the IELTS delivered by our specialist staff to help you achieve the right standards

The programmes offered are externally awarded by the international awarding body Pearson and recognised by our international partner, Portsmouth University:

BTEC requires an IELTS score of 5.5 for entry to a Level 4 course.

Many universities require an IELTS score of 6.0 for entry to a bachelor’s degree programme. If you wish to pursue a bachelor’s degree at university, it is essential that you achieve an IELTS score of 6.0 during your Level 4 year as you will apply for the university of your choice during you Level 5 programme.

Some border agencies require a minimum IELTS score to issue a student visa.

All our programmes and assessments are conducted in English.
